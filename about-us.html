About Our Dispensary

Majeeda Gulzar Free Dispensary was established with a vision to provide quality healthcare services to all members of our community, regardless of their financial status. Founded in 2024, we have been serving the people of Gujrat with dedication and compassion.

Our journey began with a small clinic and has grown into a full-fledged medical facility offering a range of services. We believe that access to healthcare is a fundamental right, not a privilege, and this belief drives everything we do.

Since our founding in 2024, we have treated thousands of patients, provided Nominal fee medications, and offered preventive health education to improve community wellbeing.

Our Core Objectives

Compassionate Care

To provide medical services with empathy, dignity and respect for all patients, understanding that healing begins with compassionate care.

Free Access

To ensure that quality healthcare remains completely free of charge for those who cannot afford medical treatment elsewhere.

Community Health

To improve overall community health through preventive care, health education, and early intervention programs.
