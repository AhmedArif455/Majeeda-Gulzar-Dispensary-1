Understanding ECG (Electrocardiogram)

An ECG machine is a powerful diagnostic tool that measures the electrical activity of the heart. It helps identify heart rhythm issues, monitor cardiac conditions, and detect early signs of cardiovascular diseases.

At Majeeda Gulzar Free Dispensary, we offer ECG services to ensure timely and accessible heart health monitoring for all patients.

A visual showing an ECG waveform on screen.

Why ECG Monitoring Matters

Detecting heart irregularities early can save lives. ECG plays a crucial role in:

Heart Rhythm Detection

ECGs detect arrhythmias like atrial fibrillation, bradycardia, and tachycardia with precision.

Heart Attack Diagnosis

ECG results are vital in identifying ongoing or previous heart attacks, enabling quick intervention.

Monitoring Cardiac Treatment

Doctors use ECG to track patient response to medications, pacemakers, and heart procedures.
